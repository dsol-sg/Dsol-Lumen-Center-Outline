OUTL INE
Outline is a slender wall lamp made in aluminum and
designed by the architect Francesco Murano. The light and
frame combine to become a luminist fresco that cuts through
space: its graphic mark has an intense visual impact and
endows the wall and surrounding area with unique character.
The integrated LED sources have exceptionally high color
rendering. Available in versions with “Total White” and “Red
Light” lighting at the end. Available ﬁnishes include FT white,
FT black, and FT red.
